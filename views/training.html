Training

Training can be a difficult task for most pet owners. That is why Pets-R-Us has placed an emphasis on creating a program to make this task much easier. Training your pet is just taking the time to learn how to communicate with them for a better relationship. The bond you will forge during the training process is invaluable. Pets-R-Us want to help you and your pet create a great bond that will stand the test of time. Whether your pet needs basic obedience training, or you want to teach them fun tricks, we can offer a timeline and the tools to make it a reality. By training your pet you are providing a better lifestyle for you and them. Investing in training for your pet will allow you and your pet to be safer in any environment because of the newfound ability to communicate. To get started with your pet’s training sign up for a free account and set an appointment to meet with the Pets-R-Us certified trainer.

Meet the Trainer

Casey is a certified Pets-R-Us trainer. Aside from providing professional training for the Pets-R-Us family, Casey spends time thinking up fun tricks to teach his pet, Maximillion. They are always outdoors because Maximillion cannot get enough of green open spaces (and Casey loves them as well). Because Casey and Maximillion spend so much time together, Casey brings an expertise that cannot be found anywhere else. Besides being a great trainer, Casey loves hiking, ultimate frisbee, cheering for Pets-R-Us sponsored teams on the weekends, and playing with all of Maximillion’s favorite friends at the Pets-R-Us park.
